About


Rich Corporation is Sacramento's premier rock band. Founded in 2015, Rich Corporation began as an experimental project by their lead guitarist, Michael Schotter, and evolved into a collaborative effort to construct songs that blend genres such as jazz, rock, folk, and more.

Members of Rich Corporation include vocalist Clark Reese, lead guitarist Michael Schotter, rhythm guitarist Mark McCoubrey, and bassist David Diaz. The band is currently looking for a new drummer, as they have recently parted ways with their previous one, William Condrey.
